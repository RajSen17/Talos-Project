# Memory: Amy Barrett

## Personal Profile
Amy is a Southern California native with maternal roots running east to Jacksonville, where Grandma Frances taught her hand-lettering across a kitchen table on weekend visits. That inheritance now sits at the center of two businesses, a craft, and a quiet private worry about being the generation where the line either continues or stops. She runs on momentum and a polish she has built deliberately, with a private churn she keeps mostly out of frame, the cost of being the first in her family to stake a living on creative, screen-shaped work.

She is expressive and quick to laugh, generous with enthusiasm, oddly exacting about kerning and color, and the kind of person who gets more productive under pressure rather than less, which makes overwhelm hard for anyone to notice, including her on the bad weeks. A quiet faith threads under her days, a habit of prayer she keeps for herself and does not perform. She graduated from Northbridge College in 2023 with a BA in Communications and a minor in Digital Media, and started freelancing the week after the ceremony. Her cultural identity blends a Southern California childhood with a maternal line that still cooks the recipes Helen learned in Jacksonville, and her sense of home is layered, multilingual, and louder than the rest of town tends to credit.

## Work & Projects
**Clover Creative LLC** is Amy's California-registered single-member social media agency, founded January 2024 after six months of post-college freelancing. She works from her bedroom desk and from rotating coffee shops in City Heights, with no physical office yet. Four active retainers anchor the business: Bloom Botanicals organic skincare ($2,500/month, contact Ryan Torres), Bloom Kitchen farm-to-table restaurant ($1,200/month, contact Carla Adams), Coastal Surf School in La Jolla ($1,500/month, contact Derek Simmons), and Laura's Boutique online fashion ($1,000/month, contact Laura Mitchell in LA). Total retainer revenue is approximately $6,200/month or $74,400/year. Services span content strategy, photo and video and graphic creation, scheduling, community management, and monthly analytics. Tools run on Canva Pro, Later, HoneyBook, CapCut, and Google Workspace. Growth plan: hire a part-time content assistant by early 2027, target six to eight clients by end of 2027, and save toward a proper home office buildout.

**Lettering by Amy** is a sole proprietorship operating under Clover Creative LLC. The style is modern calligraphy with geometric layouts, intricate florals, and clean line work. Three to five bookings per month, mostly weddings, birthday parties, baby showers, and bridal events. Pricing: bridal signage packages $250 to $400, event signage $80 to $150 per piece, small custom pieces $40 to $60. Revenue approximately $800 to $1,200/month, or roughly $12,000/year. Supplies come from Blick Art Materials and Amazon. The portfolio lives on @letteringbyamy and in a physical book she carries to bridal consultations.
